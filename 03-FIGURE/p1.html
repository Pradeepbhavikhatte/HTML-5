Ganesha is a very popular god in Hindu Religion, and is one of the most worshipped. Hindu tradition states that Ganesha is a god of wisdom, success and good luck. He is also the giver of different types of favors. The Hindu tradition calls Ganesha as the Vighneshvara. Ganesha is a Hindu god with the head of an elephant and the body of a human, and is known for removing obstacles and bringing good luck:Ganesha's elephant head represents the supreme self, or atman, while his human body represents the physical world, or maya.
